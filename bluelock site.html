Lock (Japanese: ブルーロック, Hepburn: Burū Rokku) is a Japanese manga series written by Muneyuki Kaneshiro and illustrated by Yusuke Nomura. It has been serialized in Kodansha's Weekly Shōnen Magazine since August 2018. An anime television series adaptation by Eight Bit premiered in October 2022.
Blue Lock won the 45th Kodansha Manga Award in the shōnen category in 2021.
